Co-design to increase youth active participation in civic life

"The project aims to make public communication for young people more effective and findable, and so to increase their social inclusion and active participation in civic life. Following the Living Lab approach and in the realm of user-centered design, the project builds new solutions and strives to engage youth into partecipatory process, to co-create new concepts, to explore related scenarios and usages, and to assess new technologies and early-stage artifacts."

Istituto Superiore Mario Boella
